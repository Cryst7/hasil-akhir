About Us

Fermé is a premium yoghurt brand dedicated to preserving the art of traditional fermentation. Our products are crafted in small batches using locally-sourced organic milk, bringing you unparalleled flavor, richness, and gut health benefits. Our mission is to combine wellness with refinement — simple indulgence done right.

Taste the difference of slow-crafted tradition. Yaourt Fermé is a premium yogurt made from pure milk and active cultures, fermented gently to achieve a luxuriously thick texture and clean, tangy taste. Inspired by classic French methods, it's a refined take on natural dairy. No additives. No shortcuts. Just quality ingredients and patience in every bottle. Enjoy it plain, or pair it with fruit, granola, or honey for a moment of wholesome indulgence. Simple, honest, and deliciously real.
